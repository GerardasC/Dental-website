WELCOME TO LC DENTAL CLINIC

We are pleased to welcome you to our private dental practice. LC DENTAL CLINIC is a modern and comfortable dental office committed to helping everyone in your family have a healthy smile and better quality of life. Our clinic is located in an easy-to-reach and calm part of Waterford City.

We strongly believe in the importance of preventative dentistry and are assured that a healthy and beautiful smile is within reach of every person. Our goal is to provide the finest dental care for all family members in the most comfortable and relaxing environment. We know how to change the way you feel about going to the dentist!

LC Dental Clinic offers a wide range of dental services, from tooth filling to tooth replacement. We are here to help you get the dental care you deserve!
